Crystal Collector

    Instructions ~

  1. You will be chosen a random number at the start of the game.
  2. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  3. You win if you can match your total score to the random number, you lose if your total score goes above the random number.
  4. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
  5. Each time the game starts, the value of each crystal will change.
